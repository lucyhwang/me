Wampus Waffles

Wampus Waffles is a promising late-night waffle delivery startup located in the heart of West Campus, founded by a diverse team of ambitious women. The waffle women behind Wampus Waffles strive to bring you midnight comfort food with speedy and inexpensive delivery. The Wampus Waffle Company aims to solve the problems of inconvenient business times of food places that close early, expensive food items, and increasing accessibility to food for college students.

I manage the business side of Wampus Waffles. I set the profit margins, customer segments, data analytics, launch dates, and business strategy
